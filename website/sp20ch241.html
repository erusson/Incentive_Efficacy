The $3 Spacebar Challenge

Welcome to our experiment! We are graduate students at UC Berkeley (LinkedIn profile of yours truly) running an experiment with the goal of collecting spacebar clicks from internet users. If you're reading this, we would like to give you $3 to donate to any charitable organization of your choice. All you have to do to earn this reward is click your computer's spacebar 400 times. I know it sounds like a lot, but give it a shot and watch how fast those clicks rack up! A popup will appear when you have reached the target number of clicks.

Please do not refresh the page.

Thank you for completing our experiment!

Please enter your email, the name of whatever charity you'd like to give money to, and the charity's website! At the conclusion of this experiment in April, we will email you a receipt for the $3 donation to be made under your name.

Please do not repeat or share this experiment with anybody.

Due to the nature of our experiment, our results will be ruined if you repeat the experiment or have a friend take it. If we detect that you have shared this page or repeated the experiment, we will cancel your reward. We would appreciate if you would help us get a good grade in our statistics class! :)

There's no catch -- all you have to do is click the spacebar! We will not collect any personal information from you unless you complete the challenge, but that's only so we can process and confirm your reward.

Once you reach 400 clicks, a box will appear for you to input your email address and information so that we can process the $3 reward. If you complete the form, we will send you a confirmation email, and we'll process the reward by no later than May 1st, 2020. We'll be doing all the emailing and payments by hand, so thank you for your patience!
